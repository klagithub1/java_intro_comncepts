Skills for success in university study

This credit course is an introduction to the critical elements needed to achieve success in university-level courses, such as general study skills, motivation and writing skills, as well as information literacy. Topics include learning and motivation, time management, reading strategies, organizing content, group projects, and communication skills. Information literacy topics include locating, evaluating and leveraging information effectively and ethically. More
